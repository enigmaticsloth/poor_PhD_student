Poor Ph.D. Student

I’m a bioengineering PhD student surviving on a stipend that barely covers my basic living expenses. Many times, I struggle to afford meals, my bank account runs dry, and paying rent becomes a major challenge. The financial pressure has forced me to work excessive hours and stay up late just to keep up with my research. However, I remain committed to finishing my degree and making meaningful contributions to biomedical science.

My research focuses on lung-on-a-chip technology, integrating organ-on-a-chip systems with lung stem cells to study lung development, regeneration, and to develop new therapeutic strategies for lung adenocarcinoma.
